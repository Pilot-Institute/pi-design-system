## **Effective Thumbnail Types & Examples**

### **Core Thumbnail Categories:**

### **1\. The Comparison Thumbnail**

* Split-screen showing clear contrast  
* Before/after results  
* Expected vs reality scenarios  
* Product A vs Product B matchups

**Example:** "DJI Mavic 3 vs Air 3"

* Left side shows Mavic 3 with small "2X PRICE" text  
* Right shows Air 3 with "SAME RANGE?" question  
* Creates immediate curiosity about value proposition

### **2\. The Revelation Thumbnail**

* Highlights unexpected finding  
* Uses question marks effectively  
* Shows moment of discovery  
* Creates information gap

**Example:** "The Truth About Part 107"

* Simplified FAA badge with question mark  
* "NOBODY TELLS YOU THIS" text  
* Creates curiosity about hidden knowledge

### **3\. The Problem-Solution Thumbnail**

* Shows clear pain point  
* Hints at resolution  
* Uses visual metaphors  
* Creates hope/desire

**Example:** "Stop Failing Checkrides"

* Red X over traditional study methods  
* Green checkmark with new approach  
* Promises specific solution

### **4\. The Numbers-Based Thumbnail**

* Features compelling statistic  
* Shows clear comparison  
* Uses data visualization  
* Creates immediate impact

**Example:** "Drone Range Test Results"

* "2.4X FARTHER" in large text  
* Simple distance comparison graphic  
* Makes viewer question assumptions

### **5\. The Warning/Alert Thumbnail**

* Highlights critical information  
* Uses caution elements sparingly  
* Shows clear stakes  
* Creates urgency

**Example:** "5 Illegal Drone Mistakes"

* Warning symbol with drone silhouette  
* "$10,000 FINE" text  
* Makes consequences clear
