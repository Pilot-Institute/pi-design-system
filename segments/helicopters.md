# Segment: Helicopters

Covers helicopter pilot training and related certification courses.

## Primary Color Override

| Semantic Role | Token Name | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| `--color-segment-primary` | Electric Indigo | `#2E4292` |
| `--color-segment-secondary` | Slate Blue | `#212353` |

All other tokens inherit from `design-system/DESIGN.md`.

Note: Electric Indigo is the closest match in `PI-Colour-Palette.csv` to the dark indigo used in team communications for this segment. If the team formalises a different color, update this file and the CSV accordingly.

## Segment Characteristics

- **Photography**: Helicopters in flight, rotor details, helicopter cockpit interiors, landing pads
- **Tone**: Precise, technical, serious — helicopter training is demanding and this segment reflects that
- **Illustration accent**: Electric Indigo elements against dark navy or white backgrounds
