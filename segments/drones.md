# Segment: Drones

Covers drone pilot training — Part 107 certification, commercial drone operations, drone photography, and UAS-related courses.

## Primary Color Override

| Semantic Role | Token Name | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| `--color-segment-primary` | Magenta Pink | `#F80590` |
| `--color-segment-secondary` | Fuchsia | `#E42A84` |

All other tokens inherit from `design-system/DESIGN.md`.

## Segment Characteristics

- **Photography**: Drone hardware, aerial shots, outdoor environments, drone operators in field settings
- **Tone**: Technical, precise, but approachable — modern and kinetic
- **Illustration accent**: Magenta Pink figures and icons; drone silhouettes and aerial perspective imagery

## Notes

Pink is the visual identity marker for all drone content across every output type — thumbnails, IG images, emails, ads, and video.
