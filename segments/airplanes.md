# Segment: Airplanes

Pilot Institute's flagship segment. Covers fixed-wing aircraft training — Private Pilot, Instrument Rating, Commercial Pilot, Flight Instructor, and related courses.

## Primary Color Override

| Semantic Role | Token Name | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| `--color-segment-primary` | Sky Blue | `#2398FF` |
| `--color-segment-secondary` | Bright Blue | `#1877F2` |

All other tokens inherit from `design-system/DESIGN.md`.

## Segment Characteristics

- **Photography**: Fixed-wing aircraft, cockpit interiors, runways, instructors in headsets, aerial landscape shots
- **Tone**: Authoritative, structured, aspirational — the professional path to becoming a pilot
- **Illustration accent**: Sky Blue figures and icons against dark navy or white backgrounds

## Notes

This is the default segment. When no segment is specified in a prompt, apply Airplanes color tokens.
