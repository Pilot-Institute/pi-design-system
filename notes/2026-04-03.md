#### 2. Logo visual center note

Added to DESIGN.md Section 3 and Instructions.md: the icon is asymmetrical — the Magenta Pink wings extend left, so the **visual center runs through the middle of the shield**, not the bounding box midpoint. Aligning to the bounding box center will make the logo appear shifted to the right. All layout tools (Figma, CSS, After Effects) should use the shield axis for optical centering.

#### 3. Logo SVG off-palette colors documented

Two hex values in the official logo SVG files do not appear in `PI-Colour-Palette.csv`:
- `#252e73` — dark logo wordmark (closest CSV match: Electric Indigo `#2E4292`)
- `#b8deff` — light logo variant element (closest CSV match: Baby Blue `#81D1F4`)

These are pre-tokenized legacy values locked into the SVG artwork. They must not be used as design tokens outside the logo files. Documented in DESIGN.md Section 3, both preview files, README.md, and Instructions.md.
